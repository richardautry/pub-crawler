ESB Extra Special / Strong Bitter

5.5% ABV
30 IBU
Ardent Craft Ales
Richmond, VA
More Info ▸

We’re reaching back to beer’s British roots with our newest release: ESB! Extra Special Bitter is a traditional English pale ale, and we brewed our ESB to style with 100% English ingredients. Maris Otter malt provides a classic toasty, nutty maltiness, while Challenger & East Kent Golding hops impart a spicy, earthy quality and moderate finishing bitterness. This crystal-clear, copper-colored ESB is medium-bodied and lightly carbonated, invoking the quintessential British pub experience. Cheers!
